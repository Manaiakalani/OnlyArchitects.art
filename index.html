About

We are not just drafting connections — we are constructing camaraderie.

Founded by two friends, Max and Chris, with a shared passion for architecture and meaningful relationships, OnlyArchitects is where form meets function in the social world.

Inspired by the artistry and collaboration behind every design, we set out to create a community where architects can connect, share ideas, and build relationships as strong as their blueprints.

Whether you are into sleek modernism, sustainable design, or crafting a concrete jungle — OnlyArchitects is your arch-support for collaboration. Let us design something extraordinary, together.
